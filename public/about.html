About EZPC

EZPC is a guidance-first PC part picker. It is built for people who want to understand why a component fits their goals, not just fill slots in a parts list. The site brings component research, build planning, and practical buying guidance into one place.

How EZPC helps

EZPC uses real benchmark data and price-versus-performance comparisons to make tradeoffs easier to see. Component guides explain the specifications that matter, while compatibility checks help identify common mismatches before purchase.

The guided build wizard turns a budget and a few practical questions into a starting build. You can then refine the recommendation, compare alternatives, review the balance of the system, and share the resulting build. Recommendations are a research aid, so current retailer and manufacturer information should always be verified before buying.

Why guidance comes first

A good PC build is not simply the most expensive collection of parts. Workload, display resolution, upgrade plans, power, thermals, and budget all affect the right choice. EZPC is designed to expose those tradeoffs in plain language and help each dollar contribute to the performance that matters for the intended use.

How EZPC makes money

EZPC is free to use. The site may earn affiliate revenue when a visitor follows an Amazon product link and completes a qualifying purchase. Affiliate revenue supports operation and continued development of the database, benchmark views, comparison tools, and guided builder.
