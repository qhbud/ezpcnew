Privacy Policy

Effective June 20, 2026

EZPC is designed to work without user accounts or logins. This policy explains what information the site stores when you create, save, or share a PC build and when you use links to retailers.

Builds saved in your browser

Your working and saved builds are kept in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device unless you choose to create a shared build link. You can remove browser-stored builds by using your browser's site-data controls.

Shared build references

When you choose to share a build, EZPC may save an anonymous reference record on the server so the shared link can load later. That record contains component IDs only. It does not contain your name, email address, account information, or other personal profile data. EZPC does not require accounts or logins to save or open shared builds.

Amazon affiliate links

Some product links lead to Amazon. Amazon and its service providers may collect information under Amazon's own privacy policies when you follow those links or make a purchase. EZPC does not receive your Amazon account credentials or payment details.

Privacy-friendly analytics

EZPC may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate site usage, such as page visits and general referral sources. The intended analytics setup does not use advertising profiles or cross-site tracking cookies. If the analytics provider or practices materially change, this policy will be updated.

Contact

For privacy questions, requests, bugs, or suggestions, email easypcworlddev@gmail.com. Please do not send passwords, payment information, or other sensitive data.

As an Amazon Associate EZPC earns from qualifying purchases.
